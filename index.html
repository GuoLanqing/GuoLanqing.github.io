About me

I'm a final-year Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, supervised by Professor Bihan Wen. My research interest is to study image/video restoration and enhancement, image generation, machine learning and optimization.

Prior to that, I received my B.Eng degree (2015-2019) in Software Engineering from Wuhan University.
